ABOUT US

Meet Dr. Sarah Chen

Dr. Sarah Chen graduated from the University of Toronto Faculty of Dentistry in 2012 and has been serving the Bloor West Village community for over 12 years. She believes every patient deserves gentle, thorough care in a comfortable environment.

Her approach focuses on preventive education and making dentistry accessible for families. From a child's first dental visit to cosmetic work for adults, Dr. Chen and our team are here for every stage of your family's dental health.
